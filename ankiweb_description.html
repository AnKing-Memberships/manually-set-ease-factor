This add-on will let you manually set the ease of a single or multiple selected notes. This can be done from the Browser or the Reviewer. You must be on Anki 2.1.36+ to use this add-on How to Use In the browser: Right click on one or multiple cards and choose "Set Ease Factor" In the reviewer: Click on "More" and then "Set Ease Factor" Keep in mind the default Ease is 250%.
